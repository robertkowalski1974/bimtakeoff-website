Industrial Project Types

Understanding Manufacturing Facility Complexity

Light Manufacturing

Size: 2,000-5,000 sqm
Type: Assembly & light production
Complexity: Medium
Timeline: 5-6 days
Typical Cost: £1,200-1,800/sqm

Characteristics: - Standard industrial portal frames - Moderate floor loading (300-500 kg/sqm) - Basic 3-phase power - Standard HVAC & lighting - Loading docks & yard space - Office & amenity areas

Heavy Manufacturing

Size: 5,000-20,000 sqm
Type: Metal working & fabrication
Complexity: High
Timeline: 7-9 days
Typical Cost: £1,800-2,800/sqm

Characteristics: - Reinforced structures - High floor loads (500-1,000+ kg/sqm) - Overhead crane systems (5-50 tonne) - Heavy power distribution - Compressed air & process utilities - Environmental controls - Waste treatment systems

Process & Pharmaceutical

Size: 5,000-30,000+ sqm
Type: Food, pharma, chemical processing
Complexity: Very High
Timeline: 9-10 days
Typical Cost: £2,500-4,500/sqm

Characteristics: - Clean room environments - Controlled temperature/humidity - Process piping & vessels - Automated material handling - Quality control labs - Strict regulatory compliance - Extensive MEP integration

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What makes industrial estimation different from commercial construction?

A: Industrial facilities require 30-40% higher structural capacity due to machinery loads, overhead cranes (up to 50+ tonnes), and heavy floor loading (500-1,000+ kg/sqm). MEP systems are more complex with process utilities (compressed air, process water, waste treatment) and power demands 3-5x higher than commercial buildings. Traditional estimation often underestimates these specialized requirements.

Q: Can you estimate facilities with overhead crane systems?

A: Yes, we have extensive experience costing overhead crane installations from 5-tonne jib cranes to 50+ tonne bridge cranes. Our estimates include crane runway beams, support structures, electrical distribution, controls, and installation complexity. We verify structural capacity requirements with the crane specifications.

Q: How do you handle heavy floor loading requirements?

A: We systematically analyze floor loading zones and design appropriate foundation and slab systems. For loads exceeding 500 kg/sqm, we calculate reinforcement requirements, thicker slabs, and enhanced foundation systems. Our structural cost modeling accounts for machinery vibration isolation where required.

Q: Can you cost process utility systems like compressed air?

A: Absolutely. We quantify all process utilities including compressed air generation & distribution, process water systems, waste treatment, chemical storage, and specialized drainage. Our estimates reflect industrial-grade specifications and the complexity of integrating utilities with production equipment.

Q: Do you include production equipment in estimates?

A: We focus on building fabric, structural systems, MEP infrastructure, and material handling equipment (cranes, conveyors). Production machinery is typically excluded but we include all building modifications required for equipment installation (foundations, services connections, access platforms). We clarify scope boundaries at project start.

Q: Can you model phased construction to minimize production downtime?

A: Yes, we regularly prepare phased delivery estimates that minimize operational disruption. We can model construction sequences, temporary services, partial facility operation, and the cost impact of working around active production areas. This is critical for facility expansions and modernization projects.

Q: How quickly can you assess the cost of adding crane capacity?

A: For facility upgrades like adding crane systems or increasing capacity, we can typically provide costs within 3-4 days. This includes structural modifications, crane equipment, electrical distribution, and installation complexity. Our 5D BIM workflow allows rapid assessment of modification impacts.

Q: What’s your fee structure for industrial projects?

A: Fees vary based on facility type, size, and complexity. As a guide: - Light manufacturing (2,000-5,000 sqm): £8,000-15,000 - Heavy manufacturing (5,000-15,000 sqm): £15,000-28,000 - Large industrial (15,000-30,000 sqm): £28,000-45,000 - Process/pharmaceutical (any size): Premium pricing due to complexity

Express service carries a 25% premium. Contact us for a specific quote.
