Drylining & Partitions

  • Acoustic treatments and STC/Rw ratings
  • Fire-rated partitions and compartmentation

Our Experience

  • 200+ drylining projects estimated (offices, warehouses, residential)
  • Worked with Knauf, British Gypsum, CSR Gyprock specifications
  • Understand labor rates: crew sizing, productivity factors, site access constraints

Typical Package Scope

Fire Protection Systems

What We Cover

  • Fire-rated doors, windows, and penetrations
  • Fire curtains and smoke control systems
  • Structural fire protection (steel/concrete coatings)

Our Experience

  • 150+ fire protection packages (warehouses, data centers, high-rise)
  • Compliance with AS 1530.4, BS 476, EN 13501, Polish regulations
  • Worked with Trafalgar, Hilti, Nullifire, FireMaster, Rockwool, Titan systems
  • Understand certification requirements: 3rd party testing, BCA/NCC compliance
  • Know labor complexity: confined spaces, sequential installation, coordination

Typical Package Scope

  • Coordination with other trades: critical for sequencing

Deliverable: Fire protection cost estimate with compliance roadmap

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What makes specialist trade estimating different from general estimating?

A: Specialist estimators have hands-on installation experience and understand the nuances that affect costs: fire-rated systems require specific installation sequences, facade work is weather-dependent, and structural connections have hidden complexity. We account for factors that generic estimators often miss, resulting in ±3-5% accuracy versus ±15-20% for general estimates.

Q: Do you work with specific material systems only?

A: No. We have experience with all major systems (Knauf, Gyproc, Siniat, Kingspan, Trespa, Hilti, etc.) and can estimate any manufacturer’s products. Our expertise is in understanding the installation and performance characteristics, not brand loyalty. We often provide value engineering alternatives using different systems.

Q: Can you estimate supply-only packages?

A: Yes. We provide estimates for supply-only, install-only, or complete supply & install packages. Each requires different considerations for waste factors, logistics, and risk allocation. Supply-only typically includes 5-8% waste, while install packages factor 10-15% waste depending on complexity.

Q: How do you handle compliance requirements?

A: We budget for all testing, certification, and approval costs from the start. This includes fire ratings (AS 1530.4, BS 476, EN 13501), structural load testing, facade performance testing, and authority approvals. These costs, often 3-8% of package value, are frequently missed in generic estimates.

Q: What information do you need to provide an estimate?

A: Ideally: architectural drawings, specifications, material preferences, and any performance requirements (fire ratings, acoustic performance, wind loads). For complex projects, we may request structural drawings and coordination plans. We can work with preliminary information and refine as details become available.

Q: Do you provide value engineering alternatives?

A: Absolutely. Every specialist estimate includes value engineering recommendations: alternative material systems, installation methods, and performance-equivalent substitutions that can reduce costs without compromising quality. Typical savings opportunities range from 10-20% of package value.

Q: What project sizes do you handle?

A: We work on projects from small fit-outs (500 sqm) to large-scale developments (50,000+ sqm). Specialist trade complexity matters more than overall project size—a high-rise facade is more complex than a large warehouse drylining package. Minimum fee applies for projects under €500,000.

Q: Do you support tendering processes?

A: Yes. We provide full tender support: quantity take-off, detailed cost breakdown, risk analysis, bid document preparation, and contractor evaluation. Our estimates give you confidence in your tender submissions. We can also attend tender interviews and negotiations.

Q: How quickly can you provide an estimate?

A: Standard timeline is 2-3 weeks for comprehensive specialist estimates. For urgent requirements, we offer fast-track service (5-7 working days) with a 30% surcharge. Initial budget estimates can be provided within 48 hours for feasibility studies.

Q: Do you work internationally?

A: Yes. While based in Poland, we have extensive experience with EU, UK, and Australian standards. We’ve completed projects across Europe, Asia-Pacific, and the Middle East. Our estimates include location factors for labor and material costs.
