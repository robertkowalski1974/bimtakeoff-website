### Commercial-Specific Elements

**Building Types Covered:**
1. **Small-to-Medium Office** (1,000-5,000 sqm) - £2,200-3,200/sqm
   - 3-6 storeys
   - Standard specifications
   - Natural ventilation or FCUs
   
2. **Grade A Office** (5,000-20,000 sqm) - £2,800-4,200/sqm
   - RC frame, 6-15 storeys
   - Premium specifications
   - VRV/VRF systems
   - Raised floors, suspended ceilings
   
3. **Mixed-Use Development** (10,000-50,000+ sqm) - £2,500-4,500/sqm
   - Multiple use types
   - Complex interfaces
   - Shared services
   - Phased delivery

**Cost Breakdown Structure:**
- Structure & Envelope: 40-45% (£1,090-1,690/sqm)
- Internal Fit-Out: 20-25% (£260-420/sqm)
- MEP Services: 25-30% (£490-780/sqm)
- Other Elements: 10-15% (£690-1,170/sqm)

**Key Commercial MEP Systems:**
- VRV (Variable Refrigerant Volume) air conditioning
- VRF (Variable Refrigerant Flow) systems
- Displacement ventilation
- BMS (Building Management Systems)
- Advanced lighting controls
- IT and communications infrastructure

**Commercial-Specific Terminology:**
- **Grade A Office** - Premium specification office space commanding highest rents
- **Shell & Core** - Building structure and central services only
- **CAT A Fit-Out** - Landlord fit-out to lettable standard (raised floors, ceiling, basic services)
- **CAT B Fit-Out** - Tenant fit-out (partitions, furniture, branding)
- **Floor-to-Floor Height** - Critical for commercial office value (typically 3.6-4.2m)
- **Net Internal Area (NIA)** - Lettable floor area
- **Gross Internal Area (GIA)** - Total floor area including cores
- **Efficiency Ratio** - NIA/GIA ratio (typically 80-85% for good office design)
