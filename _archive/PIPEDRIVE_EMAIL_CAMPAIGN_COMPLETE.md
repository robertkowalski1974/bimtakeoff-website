## ? TROUBLESHOOTING

### Problem: Low Open Rates (<20%)

**Solutions:**
1. **Subject line testing:**
   - Try more personalization: {{organization.name}}
   - Use numbers: "30% oszczeþdnos«ci"
   - Ask question: "Czy wasz przetarg sieþ uda??"

2. **Sender reputation:**
   - Check SPF/DKIM/DMARC records
   - Warm up new domain (slow ramp-up)
   - Use personal email domain (nie @gmail.com)

3. **Timing:**
   - Test different send times
   - Avoid Mondays/Fridays
   - 9-11am optimal

---

### Problem: High Bounce Rate (>5%)

**Solutions:**
1. **Email validation:**
   - Use email verification tool (Hunter.io, NeverBounce)
   - Remove invalid emails immediately
   - Update old records

2. **List hygiene:**
   - Remove hard bounces immediately
   - Re-verify contacts quarterly
   - Segment by engagement (active vs inactive)

---

### Problem: Low Response Rate (<5%)

**Solutions:**
1. **Value proposition:**
   - Make offer more concrete
   - Add social proof (case studies)
   - Reduce friction (easier CTA)

2. **Personalization:**
   - Research each prospect (5 min)
   - Reference specific projects
   - Show you understand their pain

3. **Follow-up:**
   - Increase touchpoints (4 ? 6 emails)
   - Add phone calls (for Segment A)
   - Multi-channel (email + LinkedIn)

---

### Problem: Spam Complaints

**Solutions:**
1. **Permission:**
   - Only email opted-in contacts
   - Clear unsubscribe link in every email
   - Honor unsubscribes within 24h

2. **Content:**
   - Avoid spam trigger words
   - Proper text/image ratio (80/20)
   - No misleading subject lines

3. **Technical:**
   - Proper SPF/DKIM/DMARC
   - Reputable ESP (Pipedrive OK)
   - Monitor sender reputation (Google Postmaster)
