Our Triple Validation System: Unmatched Accuracy

Why Three Validation Levels?

Construction projects cannot afford measurement errors. Our proprietary triple validation system ensures every quantity is verified through multiple independent processes, catching errors that single-validation systems miss.

Level 1: AI Extraction

Automated BIM Analysis

Our AI algorithms automatically extract quantities from your BIM model:

  • Pattern recognition for element identification
  • Automated measurement calculations
  • Clash detection and resolution
  • Duplicate element removal
  • Version control tracking

Accuracy: 98%
Duration: 1-2 hours

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What if my project doesn’t have a BIM model?

A: We can work with 2D drawings (DWG, PDF) but timelines will be significantly longer as we’ll need to extract quantities manually. For best results and fastest turnaround, we recommend using BIM models or consider our BIM modeling services to convert your 2D drawings first.

Q: How accurate is your triple validation system?

A: Our triple validation system achieves 99.5%+ accuracy when the source BIM model is complete and accurate. Each quantity is independently verified three times (AI extraction, expert review, cross-reference), ensuring the highest level of precision in the industry. We provide complete audit trails for all measurements.

Q: What happens if the design changes during the project?

A: That’s the beauty of BIM-based take-offs! We can quickly update quantities based on the revised model - typically within 4-8 hours rather than weeks for manual re-measurement. We offer ongoing update services at discounted rates for active projects.

Q: Do you comply with international measurement standards?

A: Yes, we comply with RICS New Rules of Measurement (NRM), ISO 19650 BIM standards, and can adapt to local standards including Polish KNR, Australian AIQS, or any other regional requirements. Our Quantity Surveyors are experienced with international projects.

Q: Can you handle large, complex projects?

A: Absolutely. We’ve successfully completed take-offs for projects ranging from small renovations to 100,000+ sqm developments. Our AI-powered system actually performs better on larger projects where manual take-offs would be prohibitively time-consuming. Complex projects may require 3-4 days instead of 2-3.

Q: What level of detail (LOD) do you need in the BIM model?

A: We can work with LOD 300 and above for most projects. LOD 350-400 is ideal for detailed trade-specific take-offs. If your model is at LOD 200, we can still extract useful quantities but may need to supplement with 2D drawings for detailed measurements.

Q: Do you provide take-offs for specific trades or disciplines?

A: Yes! We can extract quantities for any trade: structural (concrete, steel, masonry), architectural (finishes, doors, windows), MEP (mechanical, electrical, plumbing, fire protection), civil works, and specialized systems. We tailor the BOQ format to match your trade requirements.

Q: How do you handle model inconsistencies or errors?

A: This is exactly why we use triple validation. Our Level 1 AI identifies clashes and duplicates automatically. Level 2 expert review catches model errors and cross-checks with 2D drawings. Level 3 verification ensures logical consistency. We document all assumptions and flag any areas of concern for your review.

Q: What’s your pricing structure?

A: Each project is assessed individually based on project size, complexity, level of detail required, and specific deliverables. Contact us with your project details for an accurate quote within 24 hours.
