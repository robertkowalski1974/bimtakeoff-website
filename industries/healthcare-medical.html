Healthcare Project Types

Understanding Healthcare Facility Complexity

Community Clinic

Size: 500-2,000 sqm
Type: Primary care
Complexity: Medium
Timeline: 5-6 days
Typical Cost: £2,800-3,800/sqm

Characteristics: - GP consultation rooms - Basic diagnostic facilities - Limited medical gas systems - Standard HVAC requirements - Reception & waiting areas - Pharmacy & treatment rooms

Day Surgery Center

Size: 2,000-5,000 sqm
Type: Outpatient surgical
Complexity: High
Timeline: 7-8 days
Typical Cost: £3,500-5,000/sqm

Characteristics: - 2-6 operating theaters - Recovery & pre-op areas - Medical gas systems (full) - HVAC with HEPA filtration - Sterilization department - Imaging & diagnostics

Acute Care Hospital

Size: 10,000-50,000+ sqm
Type: Full-service hospital
Complexity: Very High
Timeline: 9-10 days
Typical Cost: £4,000-6,500/sqm

Characteristics: - Multiple operating theaters - ICU & HDU departments - Emergency department - Complex MEP systems - Medical imaging suites - Laboratory facilities - Inpatient wards - Central sterilization

Key Cost Drivers in Healthcare Projects

Typical Acute Hospital Breakdown (per sqm)

MEP Services (40-50%)

  • HVAC with HEPA & controls: £600-900/sqm
  • Medical gas systems: £400-650/sqm
  • Electrical & backup power: £350-550/sqm
  • Fire protection & alarms: £180-280/sqm
  • BMS & nurse call systems: £220-350/sqm
  • Subtotal: £1,750-2,730/sqm

Structure & Envelope (25-30%)

  • Foundations & substructure: £320-480/sqm
  • RC frame & floors: £450-650/sqm
  • External walls & cladding: £300-450/sqm
  • Roofing & waterproofing: £120-180/sqm
  • Subtotal: £1,190-1,760/sqm

Internal Fit-Out (15-20%)

  • Clinical finishes & flooring: £280-420/sqm
  • Internal partitions: £180-280/sqm
  • Doors & ironmongery: £150-220/sqm
  • Suspended ceilings: £120-180/sqm
  • Joinery & fixtures: £200-320/sqm
  • Subtotal: £930-1,420/sqm

Other Elements (15-20%)

  • Lifts & patient hoists: £150-250/sqm
  • External works: £180-280/sqm
  • Clinical equipment prep: £220-350/sqm
  • Preliminaries (12-15%): £600-900/sqm
  • Contingency (8-10%): £380-580/sqm
  • Subtotal: £1,530-2,360/sqm

Total Typical Range: £5,400-8,270/sqm

(Acute hospital standard, excluding land, fees, and medical equipment)

NOTE: Actual costs vary significantly based on:

  • Facility type (clinic vs. acute hospital)
  • Operating theater count & complexity
  • Medical gas system requirements
  • HVAC complexity & filtration
  • HTM compliance level required
  • Clinical equipment integration

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What makes healthcare estimation different from commercial buildings?

A: Healthcare facilities cost 40-60% more than commercial buildings due to complex MEP systems (medical gases, specialized HVAC), infection control requirements, 24/7 operational demands, and stringent regulatory compliance (HTM standards). Traditional estimation often underestimates clinical engineering complexity.

Q: Can you estimate projects with HTM compliance requirements?

A: Yes, we have extensive experience with UK Health Technical Memoranda (HTM) standards including HTM 03-01 (ventilation), HTM 02-01 (medical gas), and HTM 08-03 (fire safety). We verify compliance and include all necessary specifications in our estimates.

Q: How do you handle medical gas system estimation?

A: We systematically quantify all medical gas systems (oxygen, nitrous oxide, medical air, vacuum, AGSS) including piping, outlets, alarm panels, and manifolds. Our estimates reflect the complexity of installation, testing, and HTM 02-01 compliance requirements.

Q: Can you estimate operating theater construction costs?

A: Absolutely. We have detailed knowledge of operating theater requirements including specialized HVAC (laminar flow, HEPA filtration), medical gas systems, surgical lighting, pendants, medical-grade finishes, and pressure cascade systems. We can cost different theater classifications and complexity levels.

Q: Do you include clinical equipment in your estimates?

A: We typically focus on building fabric and MEP systems, but can include allowances for clinical equipment integration (foundations, services connections, power requirements). Major medical equipment (MRI, CT scanners) is usually excluded but we clarify scope boundaries at project start.

Q: Can you cost phased hospital construction?

A: Yes, we regularly prepare estimates for phased delivery to maintain operational continuity. We can model decant requirements, temporary services, phasing logistics, and the cost impact of working within an operational healthcare environment.

Q: How quickly can you assess infection control upgrade costs?

A: For infection control upgrades (HVAC modifications, pressure cascade systems, antimicrobial surfaces), we can typically provide costs within 3-5 days. This is particularly relevant for isolation ward conversions or pandemic response facilities.

Q: What’s your fee structure for healthcare projects?

A: Fees vary based on facility type, size, and complexity. As a guide: - Community clinic (500-2,000 sqm): £6,000-12,000 - Day surgery center (2,000-5,000 sqm): £12,000-20,000 - Small hospital (5,000-15,000 sqm): £20,000-35,000 - Acute hospital (15,000+ sqm): £35,000-60,000+

Express service carries a 25% premium. Contact us for a specific quote.
