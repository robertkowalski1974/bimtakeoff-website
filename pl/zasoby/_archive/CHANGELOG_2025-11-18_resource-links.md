## Resource URLs Added

1. **Mapa drogowa** (BIM Roadmap):
   - URL: `https://www.gov.pl/web/rozwoj-technologia/mapa-drogowa-dla-wdrozenia-metodyki-bim-w-zamowieniach-publicznych`
   - Source: Polish Ministry of Development and Technology
   - Published: July 2020

2. **Szablony EIR i BEP** (EIR and BEP Templates):
   - URL: `https://www.gov.pl/web/rozwoj-technologia/cyfryzacja-procesu-budowlanego---szablony-dokumentow-bim`
   - Source: BIM Standard PL / Ministry of Development and Technology
   - Contains: Leksykon BIM, EIR templates, BEP templates, and other BIM documents

3. **Leksykon BIM** (BIM Lexicon):
   - URL: Same as templates above (included in the same document package)
   - Source: BIM Standard PL

4. **Przewodnik ISO 19650** (ISO 19650 Guide):
   - Multiple authoritative sources provided:
     - Official ISO standard: `https://www.iso.org/standard/68078.html`
     - UK BIM Framework guidance: `https://ukbimframework.org/`
     - BSI (British Standards Institution): `https://www.bsigroup.com/en-GB/iso-19650-bim/`
